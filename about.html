About

Hi, my name is Lucia Gasca, and I’m a design student at the University of San Francisco. This project is an interactive typography website created with p5.js and VS Code. Across three pages, the quote “you came exactly when you needed to” is broken apart and explored through movement, fading text, glowing letters, stars, and cursor interaction. The space-inspired theme reflects the quiet, dark, and reflective feeling of the quote, inviting the viewer to move through the words slowly and personally.
